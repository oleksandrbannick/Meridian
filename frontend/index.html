📖 Feature Guide
▶️ Auto-Monitor — Must be enabled for bots to work. Runs every 2 seconds: fills, reposts, flip stop-losses, and watch-bot management.
△ Apex Scanner — Opens scanner with your settings. Set Min Width (minimum gap in cents) and Qty, then hit Scan. Places both YES+NO limit orders at bid+1 (front of queue). Profit = gap - 2¢ when both fill. Use All Widths to run bots at every profitable width simultaneously.
◈ Meridian — Finds games with opposing-team spread lines. You bet NO on both sides — only one team can cover, so you always win at least one. Win both if the game lands in the middle.
🛡 Flip (Stop-Loss) — If one leg fills and the market moves against you (bid falls below entry-15¢, floor 55¢), the bot market-sells the filled leg to exit and cancels the other. This limits loss to ~15-25¢ per contract.
💼 Positions Tab — Your real Kalshi positions. Click Watch to attach a bot that monitors with stop-loss + take-profit targets.
📜 History → ARB view — Your arb-only performance: fill rate, P&L, width breakdown, flip analysis. Toggle BETS to see watch-bot / straight bet history separately.
