📘 What do the colors mean? ▸
These signals tell you when to deploy the bot based on the live game situation. They read the score, clock, and market price to tell you if conditions are right — ordered from strongest to riskiest below.
🟢 LOCK
Blowout (20+ pts) — deploy with full confidence
The game is effectively over. Prices are stable and anchored. Dips will recover quickly. Best possible setup. Bright green glow.
🟢 ANCHOR
Strong lead (10–19 pts) — safe to deploy
One team has a clear lead. The favorite is well-established — dips are likely to recover. Green glow.
🟡 LEAN
Moderate lead (5–9 pts) — decent setup, some risk
A lead exists but it's not insurmountable. Dips are more likely to stick. Deploy if the price is right. Gold glow.
🔵 CLOSE
Tight game (0–4 pts, early/mid) — volatile but workable
Score is close but there's still plenty of game. Arbs can still work but prices will swing more. Use wider presets. Blue glow.
🔴 DANGER
Tight + late game — bot will block deployment
Score within 5 pts in Q4 (NBA) or last 10 min of 2nd half (college). This is where catastrophic flip losses happen. The bot blocks new deploys. Red — do not override unless you have strong conviction.
⚪ EARLY
Game just started — wait for a lead to form
Too early to read the game. No score context yet. Check back after a quarter or half.
How the bot works: Posts a limit order below the favorite's bid and waits for a dip to fill it. Once filled, the favorite recovers and the underdog drops — the bot catches the other side for a locked profit at settlement. The signal tells you how stable conditions are before you deploy.

📖 Feature Guide
▶️ Auto-Monitor — Must be enabled for bots to work. Runs every 2 seconds: fills, reposts, flip stop-losses, and watch-bot management.
🔍 Arb Scanner — Opens scanner with your settings. Set Min Width (minimum gap in cents) and Qty, then hit Scan. Places both YES+NO limit orders at bid+1 (front of queue). Profit = gap - 2¢ when both fill. Use All Widths to run bots at every profitable width simultaneously.
📐 Middles — Finds games with opposing-team spread lines. You bet NO on both sides — only one team can cover, so you always win at least one. Win both if the game lands in the middle.
🛡 Flip (Stop-Loss) — If one leg fills and the market moves against you (bid falls below entry-15¢, floor 55¢), the bot market-sells the filled leg to exit and cancels the other. This limits loss to ~15-25¢ per contract.
💼 Positions Tab — Your real Kalshi positions. Click Watch to attach a bot that monitors with stop-loss + take-profit targets.
📜 History → ARB view — Your arb-only performance: fill rate, P&L, width breakdown, flip analysis. Toggle BETS to see watch-bot / straight bet history separately.
