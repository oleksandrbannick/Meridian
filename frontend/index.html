📘 What do the colors mean? ▾
These signals tell you when to deploy the bot based on the live game situation. The bot catches the favorite during a temporary dip, then grabs the other side when the price recovers. These badges read the score + clock + market price to tell you if conditions are right.
1. The border/glow around the card — colored outline around the game card. Glowing = game conditions look good for deploying.
2. The small tag in the top-right — colored tag like 🟢 ANCHOR showing the game situation at a glance.
🟢 ANCHOR
Blowout, late game — safest to deploy
One team is up 10+ in the 4th quarter or 2nd half and priced at 75¢+. The game is decided — a temporary dip will recover. Best time to deploy the bot. Green glow.
🟡 LEAN
Solid lead, late game — decent setup
A team is up 6+ late. Not a full blowout — the lead could shrink. Dips are more likely to stick, so slightly riskier. Gold glow.
🟡 EARLY ANCHOR
Big lead, but earlier in the game
Up 15+ around halftime or Q3. Looks dominant but there's a lot of game left — comebacks happen, dips might not recover as fast. Gold glow.
🔵 CLOSE
Close game — risky to deploy
Score within 5 mid/late game. Either side could win, dips may not recover. Not a recommendation — just showing you the game is tight. Blue glow.
⚪ EARLY
Game just started — wait
First quarter or first half, too early to read the game. Wait for a lead to develop. No glow.
No tag / gray border
Pregame, game over, or no clear setup. Nothing to act on.
How the bot works: The bot places a limit order below the favorite's bid and waits for a temporary dip to fill it. Once filled, the favorite's price recovers and the underdog drops — the bot catches the other side. The signal tells you whether the game state makes it safe to deploy (i.e., the favorite is likely to recover from any dip).
