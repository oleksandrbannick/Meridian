📖 Feature Guide
🔄 Refresh — Re-fetches all open sports markets from Kalshi's API. Markets are grouped by game and categorized into Winner, Spread, Total, and Player Props.
▶️ Auto-Monitor — You must enable this for the bot to work. Without it, your orders sit in the book but the bot doesn't check fills, repost stale orders, or trigger stop-losses. When enabled, it runs every 2 seconds and handles:
  • Fill detection — notices when YES or NO orders get filled
  • Stale repost — cancels old orders and re-posts at current bid (never above) to stay competitive in the queue
  • Partial fill resize — if one side partially fills, resizes the other side to match
  • Stop loss — if only one side fills and the bid drops below your entry minus the stop-loss threshold, it market-sells the filled side and cancels the unfilled order to prevent orphan positions
  • Live bid/ask — refreshes current market prices on each bot card
🔍 Arb Scanner — Scans all open sports markets for dual-arb opportunities. It finds markets where the combined YES bid + NO bid is below 100¢ — the gap is your potential profit. You set limit orders on both sides and when both fill, the profit is locked at settlement.
⚡ Dual-Arb Bot — Click any YES or NO price button on a market to open the bot builder. It places two limit orders (one YES, one NO) that together cost less than 100¢. When both fill, the contract settles at 100¢ regardless of outcome — the difference is your guaranteed profit.
📊 Player Props NO Side — Kalshi's web interface hides the NO button on player prop markets, but the API supports it. This app lets you buy NO on any player prop, giving you an edge others can't access through the website.
🔴 LIVE Filter — Shows only markets for games currently in progress. Live games have a green border and show the real-time score from ESPN.
